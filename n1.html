Narendra Modi

Prime Minister of India

Narendra Modi is an Indian politician who has been serving as the Prime Minister of India since May 26, 2014. He is a member of the Bharatiya Janata Party (BJP) and the Rashtriya Swayamsevak Sangh (RSS). Narendra Modi was born on September 17, 1950, in Vadnagar, Gujarat.

Before becoming the Prime Minister, he served as the Chief Minister of the Indian state of Gujarat from 2001 to 2014. Under his leadership, Gujarat experienced significant economic growth and development. Modi's tenure as the Prime Minister has been marked by various initiatives and policies aimed at transforming India's economy and infrastructure.

Narendra Modi is known for his charismatic leadership style and his emphasis on "Make in India," "Digital India," and "Swachh Bharat Abhiyan" (Clean India Campaign). He has played a pivotal role in shaping India's domestic and foreign policies.

Modi's leadership has had a profound impact on Indian politics, and he remains a prominent and influential figure on the global stage.
